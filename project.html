Our Projects

Community developement initiatives driven by our members across Giade LGA.

Tree Planting Phase 1 - Guest House

Completed

We successfully carried out the planting of mango trees within the Guest House, aimed at reducing the effects of desertification while enhancing the environment through shade and natural cooling. This project also serves as a long-term source of fruit and environmental sustainability.

Tree Planting Community

Schools Awareness Drive

Active

The Schools Awareness Drive is an active outreach program where we visit secondary schools across Giade LGA to educate students on cleanliness, environmental sanitation, and climate change. We highlight its causes, effects, and promote simple everyday actions that encourage a cleaner and more sustainable environment.
