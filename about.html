About Our Group

Environment & Climate Action CDS is an NYSC Community Development Service group based in Giade Local Government Area, Bauchi State, committed to environmental sustainability and climate resilience in our host communities.

🎯 Core Objectives

  • ✔ Promote environmental cleanliness and sanitation
  • ✔ Educate communities on climate change and its effects
  • ✔ Encourage tree planting and green initiatives
  • ✔ Advocate for proper waste management and recycling
  • ✔ Support sustainable practices within the community

👷 What the Group Does

  • ✔ Organizes community clean-up campaigns.
  • ✔ Conducts environmental awareness outreach in schools
  • ✔ Promotes water conservation through efficient water use awareness
  • ✔ Partners with local authorities on sanitation projects
  • ✔ Encourages environmental responsibility through behavioral change

ℹ Group Details

  • 📍 Giade LGA, Bauchi State
  • 👫 48 corps members
  • 📅 Meets every Thursday
